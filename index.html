BSc

A bachelor of science is an undergraduate degree of three years duration which is universally offered across institutes and universities across India. BSc is one of the most popular courses opted by students who have a flair for scientific aptitude and zeal for research-oriented and calculative approaches based on a proven systematic method.

Bcom

TheBachelor of commerce is designed to provide students with a wide range of managerial skills, while building competence in a particular area of business (see aside). Most universities, therefore, plan the degree such that in addition to their major, students are exposed to general business principles, taking courses in accounting, finance, economics, business management, human resources and marketing.

P.G

A POSTGRADUATE (P.G) is a student who has successfully completed an undergraduate degree level course at a college or university and is undertaking further study at a more advanced level.

Our Campus Facilities

Library

After the advent of internet, there has been an explosion of knowledge. There is no possibility of students coming up with excuses on lack of information in the ever increasing competition. Prathibha Group of Institutions understood this importance very early and established a repository of books. We have a large collection of reference books on advanced disciplines that are aimed at developing students beyond their curriculum. It not only consists of textbooks but also various newspapers, reference books and an archival of editorial clippings and important articles compiled on various interesting subjects. The college library plays a central role in helping us achieve the mission of providing students a quality education. Beyond its immediate responsibility to provide the information services and resources that support the courses of study offered at the college, the library has the broader mission of fostering in our students the importance and necessity of lifelong learning.

Laboratories

Whatever be your chosen area of specialization, computing skills will be an asset in your career. In view of this, the institution has invested and will continue to invest in the state-of-the-art computing facilities along with internet connectivity. The Institution has 6 full-fledged Computer Labs with the latest state-of-the-art Hardware and Software to facilitate the students in learning the nuances of Basic Concepts, Operating Systems, Programming, Networking, Web-Enabled Operations and Multimedia etc.

Seminar Hall

Academic excellence is only possible when teaching fraternity uses state of the art courseware in imparting course curriculum to the students. In this context, our faculty is encouraged to use the latest teaching aids like audio-visual aids, OHP, LCD Projector and Internet Connectivity. In keeping with the modern trends in the field of education, our institution provides 2 spacious and ventilated seminar halls well equipped with a captivating ambience, audio-visual aids, OHP, LCD Projector and Interactive Board facilities suitable for conducting/attending regular orientation programmes like extension lectures, guest lectures, personality development classes, e-classes, seminars etc through the power point presentations.
